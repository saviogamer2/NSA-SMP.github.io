📜 Server Rules — NSA SMP

📖 Basic Rules

  1. No hacking, cheating, or use of unfair modifications.
  2. Respect all players and staff — no harassment or bullying.
  3. No griefing or stealing from other players.
  4. Do not exploit bugs or glitches.
  5. No spamming or advertising other servers.
  6. Keep chat clean — no offensive language or inappropriate content.
  7. Listen to staff decisions — their word is final.
  8. Use common sense — play fair, have fun!

⚒️ PvP & Raiding Rules

  1. PvP is allowed in designated areas only (or if both parties agree).
  2. Base raiding is allowed unless protected by official claims.
  3. No spawn camping or unfair ganking new players.

🚫 Ban & Punishment Policy

  1. Breaking rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
  2. Ban appeals can be made on Discord or via the contact form.
